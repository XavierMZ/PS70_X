About Me

I'm a senior at Harvard College, living in Leverett house. I study Applied Mathematics specializing in Economics, as well as Philosophy. At Harvard, I'm President of Voters Choose, a student org dedicated to researching, educating, and advocating for voting reform in America. I am also an active member of the John Adams Society.

I was born in Boston in 2001, but soon after moved to Puerto Rico, where I grew up. In my free time, I like to play chess and piano, and enjoy anything and everything sci-fi related.
